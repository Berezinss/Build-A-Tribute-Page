Steve Jobs

The man who revolutionized innovation

Steve Jobs with an early Macintosh model

Timeline of Steve Jobs:

  • 1955: Born in San Francisco, adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.
  • 1972: Attended Reed College, later dropped out.
  • 1976: Co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak.
  • 1984: Launched the Macintosh computer.
  • 1985: Resigned from Apple after Macintosh's struggle.
  • 1986: Bought Pixar, which later produced blockbuster films.
  • 1997: Returned to Apple, becoming CEO.
  • 2007: Released the first iPhone, changing the tech industry.
  • 2011: Passed away from pancreatic cancer.
